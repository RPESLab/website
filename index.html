Prof. Haoyong Chen

Title: Ph.D., Professor, SM.IEEE, F.AETDEW
Office: Department of Electrical Engineering, South China University of Technology, China


Highlights

  • An influential professor of electrical engineering in both academia and industry in China
  • 24 years of research and teaching experience as a university faculty member in electrical engineering
  • Leader of the New Energy Power System Research Group at South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China
  • Director of the Institute of Power Economics and Electricity Markets at South China University of Technology
  • Chief Scientist of the Asia-Pacific Research Institute of Smart Grid and Renewable Energy (Hong Kong)
  • Founder of the Universal & Prudential Think Tank for Power and Energy (Guangdong, China)
  • Principal investigator for numerous key national projects in China
  • Maintains close collaborations with the Chinese power industry

Education

  • Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering (1997–2000), Xi’an Jiaotong University , Xi’an, Shaanxi, China
  • M.S.E. in Electrical Engineering (1995–1997), Xi’an Jiaotong University , Xi’an, Shaanxi, China
  • B.S. in Electrical Engineering (1991–1995) – Awarded with the highest honor, Xi’an Jiaotong University , Xi’an, Shaanxi, China
  • Highly-Gifted Class (1990–1991), Admitted at the age of 15 at Xi’an Jiaotong University

Professional Experience

  • Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering, South China University of Technology (Dec 2008 – Present, Guangzhou, China)
  • Associate Professor and Outstanding Young Scholar, South China University of Technology (Dec 2006 – Dec 2008, Guangzhou, China)
  • Associate Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University (Jun 2003 – Dec 2006, Xi’an, China)
  • Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering, Xi’an Jiaotong University (Jun 2000 – Jun 2003, Xi’an, China)
  • Visiting Professor, Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis (Mar 2015 – Jun 2016, USA)
  • Visiting Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering, Columbia University (Dec 2012 – Jan 2013, USA)
  • Visiting Assistant Professor, Computational Intelligence Applications Research Laboratory, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (Feb 2007 – May 2007, Hong Kong)

Key Achievements:
1) Published over 300 research papers and authored multiple books
2) Significant contributions to China’s green energy initiatives, particularly in Hainan’s Free Trade Pilot Zone
3) Recipient of prestigious awards for research excellence from the Ministry of Education and other institutions
